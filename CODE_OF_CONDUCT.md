# Code of Conduct

Maestro follows the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

Be respectful, technically rigorous, and inclusive. Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, and disclosure of private information are not acceptable.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the contact method published in the repository metadata. Maintainers will handle reports confidentially and may remove content or restrict participation when necessary.
